Snake Pattern

The Snake pattern is a great gift and a very cute friend. His stripes alternate but the pattern does not tell you when to switch colors however if you want to follow the pattern I made for the stripes I explain it later.

Materials: 5 Yarn colors: Light, Medium, and Dark Green, Red and Black yarn, Crochet Hook, Scissors, Lardge Sewing Needle.

Notes: The pattern will not tell you how to add the stripes it will just say to 'crochet around' but if you want to stripe the snake how I did here is how you do it: Crochet 32 RNDs in Medium Green. The stripes start now and continue through the DEC rounds. 6 RNDs of Black, 5 RNDs of Dark Green, 6 RNDs of Black, 2 RNDs of Light Green, repeat until the end of the pattern.

Stitches: Chain = (CH), Single Crochet = (SC), Increase = (INC), Decrease = (DEC), Slip Stitch = (SLST), Stitches = (ST).

Snake:









Weave in all of the ends and sew the button loosely onto RND 7. The last RND has a hook that will loop around the button and around your stove handle.
