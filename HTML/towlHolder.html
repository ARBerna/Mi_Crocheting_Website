Towel Holder Pattern

The Towel Holder is much more convenient then crocheting right onto a towel because this way you can switch out your towels rather than crocheting onto twenty different towels.

Materials: 2 Yarn colors: Blue and White, Crochet Hook, Scissors, Lardge Sewing Needle.

Notes: The ring I used is about six inches in diameter and I also used quite a large button which I sewed on loosely so that the loop made out of CHs fit nicely underneath it. Also since I changed colors every round there is no CH at the start of each RND, the next RND is just attatched to the last stitch.

Stitches: Chain = (CH), Single Crochet = (SC), Cluster Stitch = (CL), Cluster Decend = (DEC), Slip Stitch = (SLST), Stitches = (ST).

Special Stitches: CL = [DC 2, CH 1, DC 2], DEC = [DC 2, CH 1, DC 2 in next CH space].
